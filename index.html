Why XR Safety Training Works

  • Zero Risk, Full Exposure: Trainees can experience catastrophic failure scenarios — chemical spills, equipment malfunctions, fire emergencies — without any real-world danger.

  • Repeat on Demand: Unlike physical drills, XR scenarios can be run unlimited times, allowing trainees to practice until mastery is achieved.

  • Real-time Performance Analytics: Every movement, decision, and response is captured — enabling data-driven assessment and targeted improvement.

  • 75% Better Retention: Studies show VR-trained workers retain knowledge 75% longer than those trained with traditional methods.

  • Scalable Across Sites: Deploy the same certified training simulation to workers across multiple plant locations simultaneously.

🚢 Shipbuilding & Marine

Ship Design, Safety & Workforce Training

The shipbuilding industry demands extreme precision, coordination, and a relentless focus on safety. XR transforms how shipyards train and upskill their workforce — from virtual walkthroughs of complex hull designs and engine room layouts, to AR-guided welding and pipe-fitting procedures. Workers can rehearse confined space entry, fire and flood emergency drills, and crane operations in fully simulated environments with zero risk of real-world harm. Digital twins of vessels enable engineers to identify structural issues during design, long before steel is cut — reducing costly rework. Multiplayer XR training allows distributed teams across shipyard facilities to collaborate on safety protocols, damage control scenarios, and launch procedures simultaneously, ensuring every crew member is prepared for the critical demands of maritime operations.
